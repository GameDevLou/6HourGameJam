Create a game concept in 6 hours!

wait what...? only 6 hours? I can't make a game in 6 hours, even 48 hours is usually cutting it close for even a simple game. #6HourGameJam is not about creating a normal jam game, this is about creating a verry simple concept or single mechanic. Most importantly #6HourGameJam is to learn!

Is this a compitition?

No, just for fun.

Okay, what are the rules?

  • Everyone starts at 7pm and ends at 1am(thier local time)
  • Work solo on your game, no teams.
  • No existing/premade assets. (feel free to add those post-jam, but not in the version you submit!)
  • You own your game, you just give #6HourGameJam the right to keep it on our site.
  • You are free to make post-jam versions, make money etc You own your game.
  • Be excellent to eachother.
